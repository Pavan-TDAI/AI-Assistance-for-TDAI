## Organization Overview
TDAI is building a Personalized AI Work Assistant that helps employees, managers, and leadership automate day-to-day execution workflows. The platform is intended to become a chat-first operating layer for work coordination, reporting, meeting follow-ups, artifact generation, and business process automation.

The assistant should help teams reduce manual work, improve visibility, and make execution easier through a natural-language interface. The long-term goal is to let users ask for work outcomes in chat and receive structured results, drafts, reports, action plans, or execution help with minimal friction.

## Current Product Direction
The platform already supports:

- sending emails
- creating meetings
- generating minutes of meeting from uploaded transcripts
- sending minutes of meeting to attendees
- basic file system actions such as creating files, reading files, and summarizing file content
- reporting workflows for employee daily updates, blockers, and weekly business reports

TDAI now wants to expand the assistant into a broader business copilot that can support:

- customer outreach and email campaigns
- project proposal generation
- market benchmarking support
- digital transformation solution design
- role-based usage for employees, managers, and admins

## Business Problem
Many organizations still depend on fragmented tools, manual follow-up, inconsistent reporting, and scattered documents. Managers spend too much time collecting updates, resolving blockers through ad hoc follow-ups, preparing proposals, and building client-facing communication from scratch.

This creates several pain points:

- reporting is inconsistent across team members
- blockers are discovered late
- meeting outcomes are not captured cleanly
- proposals take too long to prepare
- customer outreach lacks repeatable structure
- transformation consulting work begins without a reusable internal blueprint

## Target Users
### Employee
- sends daily work updates
- asks for summaries, task help, drafts, and file understanding
- receives role-limited access

### Manager
- reviews team updates and blockers
- asks for WBR and team summaries
- generates customer outreach drafts
- creates proposals and strategic solution documents

### Admin
- monitors system setup and access
- manages workflows across teams
- uses advanced business artifact generation

The immediate goal is to build an outreach campaign that introduces TDAI as a practical AI work assistant for operations and transformation teams.

Desired campaign outputs:

- email sequence steps

## Proposal Generation Requirement
TDAI wants the assistant to generate proposal drafts for client-facing work. The proposal should feel business-ready and should not read like a generic AI answer.

Proposal outputs should typically include:

- executive summary
- client problem statement
- market benchmarking structure
- value proposition
- scope and deliverables
- implementation approach
- timeline
- risks and assumptions
- commercial placeholder section

Benchmark categories should include:

- speed of implementation
- reporting visibility
